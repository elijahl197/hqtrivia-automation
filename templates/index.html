One-click solver bookmarklet

Add this bookmarklet to your browser. While logged in to nytimes.com, click it once — it fetches the puzzle directly from your browser and solves it automatically. No cookie needed.

  1. Click Copy bookmarklet below.
  2. Create a new bookmark in your browser and paste it as the URL.
  3. On any nytimes.com page (while logged in), click the bookmark → puzzle solves in a new tab.
